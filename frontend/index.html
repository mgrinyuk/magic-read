How it works

From any text to active language practice

Magic Read helps learners turn real texts into listening, pronunciation, vocabulary, grammar, and writing practice.

1

Paste any text

Add a text in Chinese, Russian, Turkish, English, or German. Magic Read splits it into clear sentence cards.

2

Listen and repeat

Play each sentence, slow it down, record your voice, and get a simple pronunciation score.

3

Understand deeply

Translate sentences, click words, save flashcards, and inspect grammar inside the text.

4

Practice actively

Turn the same text into speaking games, flashcards, and printable writing practice.
